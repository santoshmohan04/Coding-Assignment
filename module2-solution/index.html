Our Menu

idli

A popular south indian breakfast recipe made with ground urad dal and idli rava batter. Served with the combination of chutney recipes and sambar recipes.

luchai puri

A traditional and authentic bengali deep fried puffed bread recipe made with plain flour. The recipe is very similar to a traditional poori recipe but just that this recipe is made with maida and ghee. The striking feature of this recipe is its texture and white bright colour which makes it attractive and appealing.

tuppa dosa

A tasty and flavoured dosa recipe made with the traditional dosa batter with a generous amount of ghee. It is basically an extension to the traditional dosa with an extra topping of clarified butter to make it more flavoured and tasty. It is generally served with a range of chutney recipes but can also be served with choice of curries for morning breakfast and also for lunch and dinner.
